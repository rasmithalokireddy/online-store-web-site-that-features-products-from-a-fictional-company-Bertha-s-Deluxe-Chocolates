Across the country, the Bertha's name is synonymous with award-winning chocolates, caramels, and their exquisite Angelfood candy. Owned and operated by brothers Don Jr., Steve, and Bertha, their family business employs 45 people year round, and up to 60 during the holidays. The entire Bertha family and team of skilled candy makers and chocolatiers are dedicated to making chocolates and confections of uncompromising quality.
